=== ANTI-AI RULES (MANDATORY) ===

BANNED OPENING PHRASES:
- "In today's rapidly evolving..."
- "It's important to note that..."
- "Let's dive into..."
- "One key aspect is..."
- "Furthermore..." (as paragraph opener)
- "In conclusion..." / "To summarize..."
- "It's worth mentioning that..."

BANNED CLOSING PATTERNS (never end a section with these):
- "...is crucial/essential/vital."
- "...should not be underestimated."
- "...plays a key role."
- Any sentence restating the section heading in different words.

- No paragraph may contain only sentences of 15-25 words.

- NEVER two consecutive sentences of similar word count.

SECTION LENGTHS (MANDATORY):
- At least one section must be 600+ words (depth).
- At least one section must be under 150 words (brevity).
- NEVER all sections at 300-400 words.

INVENTED STATISTICS (STRICTLY BANNED):
- NEVER invent percentages, timeframes, or numbers without a real source.

PUNCTUATION:
